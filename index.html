MAY THE KICK BE WITH YOU

Choose Your World

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Pick a world to start kicking in.
  2. Click and hold on the ball to start charging your shot.
  3. While holding, move the mouse left/right to aim — watch the dashed line.
  4. Release to kick. The longer you hold, the more power and arc you get.
  5. Get the ball between the posts to score — watch out for wind and world obstacles that can block your shot.
  6. Missing or getting blocked resets your streak, so chain goals for a new best score.
